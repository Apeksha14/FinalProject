Introduction

The vtac group is a diverse cadre of professionals committed to providing you up-to-date and accurate information regarding childcare and educational opportunities throughout the United States and abroad. Apeksha Hakim, Christina Ruc, Theresa Bates, and Victor Mack all hail from Charlotte, NC, a region rich in educational leadership and ground-breaking decisions from the Swann v. Board of Education case to current school assignment incorporating socio-economic status to ensure school equity.

The vtac group is committed to providing its services to the public for free to ensure all children have an opportunity to engage in a quality education, regardless of race, ethnicity, religion, gender, or background.
